Questions

The honest fine print

What exactly do I get?

Precise, per-area live-door lists — address, carrier and confirmed status — refreshed as new doors flip live in your markets, delivered as CSVs (and in your client dashboard).

Are these warm leads?

No, and we won’t pretend otherwise. They’re cold but sellable — real addresses where fiber just went live. The value is timing and precision, not a pre-warmed prospect.

How accurate is “live”?

A door is only marked live from a real, observed serviceability check showing the not-live → live flip. We never fabricate a verdict.

What does the pilot cost?

Nothing. We stand up a real area in your market so you can judge the door quality yourself before any commitment.
