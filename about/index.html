About us

We catch the one day that matters.

Neon Fiber Systems is a fiber buildout intelligence company. We find where carriers are building fiber, watch every address, and catch the exact day it goes live, so sales teams sell it first.

Why we exist

Fiber is being built faster than anyone can track by hand, and it goes live one neighborhood at a time. For a door-to-door sales rep, that timing is everything. Knock a day early and the address can’t be sold. Knock a week late and a competitor already has it. We built Neon Fiber to catch the exact day a carrier turns fiber on at an address, and to hand that to the people who sell it.

How we’re different

Most “fiber lead” lists are guesses about where service might arrive. Ours are anchored to real construction permits and confirmed by real serviceability checks. We don’t sell a maybe. We sell the doors that just became sellable, with the carrier and confirmed status attached.

What we promise

The product is only worth what you can trust, so we hold a hard line. We never fabricate a serviceability verdict or a “fiber coming” flag. Go-live dates are estimates and we label them as estimates. Every lead is anchored to a real, recent permit. And when we can’t verify an area, we say so. Honest signal is the whole product.

Where we work

We collect fiber builds nationwide and go deep where our clients sell. Our focus markets are Arizona, Colorado and Michigan, with Arizona as our first market. As clients expand, so does our coverage.
